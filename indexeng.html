Reputation in Our Field

Representing the Grilling Dynasty

Having started in 1990, our company has been introducing futuristic lines since its birth date. Developed some space in the furniture area, delivering the best quality possible, with durable products and, consequently, absorving a significant client mass. Later on, our focus turned sight to the grilling world, where we found key spaces that needed innovation coupled with some redesigned products that breath reliability. That has been our story, a company that creates proximity with the ones that share passion with our values, and highly recognises the loyalty of its clients, embracing the duty to inject progress into our service. Today, we sell for more than 10 countries (mainly in Europe), taking part on numerous events around the world and being effectively present for our contributors, the total positive feedback has been speaking by itself. We leave no one behind, be a part of our story.
